Family photo planner

Best family portrait moments

Gran Meliá Arusha

Sunrise or late afternoon in landscaped gardens. Use 70–180 mm around 85–120 mm, f/4–f/5.6, 1/500, People detection.

Tarangire

Family with baobab in background. Use 180–300 mm from a safe distance, Bank C, f/8, 1/800.

Serengeti

Vehicle-side family portrait with endless plains. Use 70–180 mm, backlight at golden hour, protect highlights.

Ngorongoro Rim

Crater backdrop at sunrise or after mist lifts. Use Bank C, f/8, 1/500, tripod or stable support if needed.

Stone Town

Use colorful doorway or textured wall. 70–180 mm, f/4, 1/500, People detection, ask before photographing others nearby.

Zanzibar Beach

Sunrise family walk or dhow silhouette. Bank B for portraits, Bank C for environmental frames. Remove CPL in low light.
