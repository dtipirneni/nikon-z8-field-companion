Photo Banks A–D

Use the same letter and purpose in photo and video. Pair each Photo Shooting Menu Bank with the matching Custom Settings Bank.

A — Safari Wildlife

High Efficiency★ RAW · M + Auto ISO · Matrix · 1/1600 s · AF-C Animals · Wide C1 / Fn1 3D override · 15 fps · 180–600mm f/5.6–6.3.

B — Birds & Action

High Efficiency★ RAW · M + Auto ISO · Matrix · 1/2500–1/3200 s · AF-C Birds · Wide C1 · 20 fps · 180–600mm f/5.6–6.3.

C — Travel / People / Environment

Lossless RAW+JPEG · Matrix · AF-C people / AF-S static · 1/500 s people · f/2.8–f/4 portraits · f/8 environment · 70–180mm f/2.8.

D — Night Sky / Low Light

Lossless RAW · M · Matrix reference · f/2.8 · ISO 3200–6400 · MF · VR Off on tripod · 5–8 s at 70mm or 1.6–2 s at 180mm · 70–180mm f/2.8.

Complete setup: Open the Camera Setup Center for paired video settings, button assignments, i Menu, My Menu and verification.
Matching letters and names

Video Banks A–D

The video bank letters mirror the photo bank purposes to reduce mistakes while switching modes.

A — Safari Wildlife

4K 60p H.265 10-bit SDR · 1/125 s · Animal detection · continuous AF · Hi-Res Zoom Off · 180–600mm.

B — Birds & Action

4K 120p when slow motion matters, otherwise 4K 60p · H.265 10-bit SDR · Bird detection · Hi-Res Zoom Off · 180–600mm.

C — Travel / People / Environment

4K 24/30p H.265 10-bit SDR · 1/50 or 1/60 s · smooth people AF · Hi-Res Zoom Off by default · 70–180mm.

D — Night Sky / Low Light

4K 24p H.265 10-bit SDR · 1/50 s · f/2.8 · manual focus or slow AF · Hi-Res Zoom Off · 70–180mm.

Front controls

Focus-mode button: hold it and rotate the rear dial for AF-S/AF-C/MF; rotate the front dial for Wide C1, Wide S, 3D tracking or Single-point.

Fn1/Fn2: programmable shortcuts. This guide uses Fn1 for 3D-tracking override and Fn2 for Recall shooting functions.

Rear controls

AF-ON: starts autofocus when using back-button focus or can be customized for focus hold.

Joystick/sub-selector: moves the AF box and confirms the chosen subject.

i button: opens the fast menu where your bank, subject detection, AF area, metering and VR should live.

Protect/zoom: useful for 100% playback focus checks.

Top controls

MODE: choose Manual exposure.

Release: select burst rate or self-timer.

ISO: change ISO; hold ISO and rotate the front dial to toggle Auto ISO.

± EV: fine-tune exposure while Auto ISO is active.

Photography fundamentals

Exposure triangle and cheat sheets

These reference images were supplied by you and are included locally in the app for offline viewing.

Shutter speed

Controls motion. Use 1/1600 s for general wildlife, 1/2500–1/3200 s for birds and fast action, and slower speeds only for intentional blur.

Aperture

Use the widest practical aperture for portraits and low light. Stop down toward f/8 for groups, habitat and greater depth of field.

ISO

In Manual exposure with Auto ISO, choose shutter and aperture for the image you want and let ISO respond to changing safari light.

Safari interpretation: Keep shutter speed and aperture under your control in Manual mode, then let Auto ISO adapt as animals move between sun and shade.

🎨 Ejaz Khan–inspired composition

Rule of thirds: place the eye near an intersection.

Negative space: leave room toward gaze or movement.

Symmetry: center only when shape and eye contact are strong.

Monochrome preview: use Deep Tone Monochrome to judge tone while retaining RAW color.
