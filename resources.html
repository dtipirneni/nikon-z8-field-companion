Quick-reference summary

Tanzania visitor guide

Use the original PDF for authoritative wording. This section is a convenient offline summary.

🛂

Passport and visa

Carry a passport with a blank visa page. U.S. and Canadian travelers require a Tanzania visa; confirm the correct visa category and current requirements before travel.

🦟

Malaria

Consult your physician or travel clinic about malaria precautions and any other destination-specific health recommendations.

🛡️

Zanzibar insurance

Zanzibar requires destination insurance for visitors. Carry a digital and offline copy of proof for every traveler and verify current requirements before departure.

💧

Water

Drink sealed bottled or otherwise safely treated water. Use the same caution for ice and brushing teeth when local guidance recommends it.

🔌

Electricity

Power is 240V, 50–60Hz. Type G and Type D plugs are used. Confirm that chargers support 100–240V.

💵

Money

Carry recent U.S. dollar notes in mixed denominations. ATMs and card acceptance can be unreliable outside major towns and hotels.

📱

Communication

Mobile coverage is generally available in towns, Zanzibar and tourist areas, but can be limited inside parks and reserves.

🕌

Zanzibar customs

Dress conservatively in public areas, especially Stone Town. Respect Islamic practices and local social traditions.

🛍️

Shopping and imports

Avoid prohibited wildlife products, plants, meat products, skins and restricted goods. Keep gemstone certificates and receipts.

♻️

Plastic bags

Single-use plastic bags are restricted. Carry reusable bags. Small resealable liquid bags should remain with the traveler.
